Our 10 Values for Better Communication

  1. 1 Empathy: Recognize that you are not the only human being on earth so be kind and Respectfull.

  2. 2 Customer Obsession: Simply put the Customer is everything.

  3. 3 Your Position: Your title makes you a Manager but the People make you a Leader.

  4. 4 Honesty: Be Real don't try to to impress or manipulate others with words.

  5. 5 Eagerness: Don't be afraid to fail if you have an idea Speak Up.

  6. 6 Trust: Don't doubt your team mates.

  7. 7 Confidentiality: They are not the companies Secrets their also yours.

  8. 8 OpenNess: Don't be afraid to reveal your thoughts and feelings to others

  9. 9 Flexiblity: Don't just be a Robot at Work come up with your own Ideas.

  10. 10 Now: You could die tomorrow so live like its your last day on Earth.
